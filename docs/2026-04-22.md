## OFFICIAL BLOGS

**Anthropic Engineering** — Preparing Your Security Program for AI-Accelerated Offense

The Takeaway: AI models are rapidly reducing the resources, time, and skill required to find and exploit vulnerabilities. Within 24 months, vast numbers of bugs that sat unnoticed for years will be found by AI and chained into working exploits.

Key recommendations: Close your patch gap immediately (patch KEV-listed vulnerabilities within 24 hours). Prepare for order-of-magnitude increases in vulnerability report volume. Add AI-assisted code review to CI pipelines. Find bugs before attackers do by scanning with the same models they'd use. Design for breach with zero-trust architecture and hardware-bound credentials. Shorten incident response time — exploits can now appear within hours of a patch.

The blog includes practical tips for each area and a useful self-check: before submitting a vulnerability report, close the editor and explain the bug from memory. If you can't describe what goes wrong without referring back to model output, you don't understand it well enough to report.
