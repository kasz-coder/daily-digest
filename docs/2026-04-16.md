## Podcasts

**Training Data** — "From SEO to Agent-Led Growth: Profound's James Cadwallader"

James Cadwallader, co-founder and CEO of ProFound (a marketing platform for the AI era serving 12% of the Fortune 500), explains the fundamental shift from traditional SEO to "Agent-Led Growth." His key insight: "The Internet has remained the same. It's just who is using the Internet" — now AI agents with "infinite bandwidth" crawl and evaluate content differently than humans.

**The Takeaway:** In the agentic paradigm, you're no longer marketing to humans — you're marketing to superintelligent agents that will consume 100x more of the Internet, meaning marketers need to create content designed entirely for agents, not humans.

Key points from the conversation:

- **The biggest misconception:** Thinking this is just SEO. The difference: in SEO, content was designed for algorithms but consumed by humans. Now content must be designed for agents to discover AND consume.

- **Agents use the long tail:** While humans focus on top 4-5 results, AI agents use orders of magnitude more sources — Cadwallader's showerhead search used 65 different web pages.

- **Platform differences matter:** Gemini leans heavily on YouTube (Google owns it), ChatGPT pulls from Reddit for consumer/B2B and LinkedIn, and Claude is updating its classifiers to use more real-time web data.

- **Content strategy rethink:** With AI-generated content now preferred by 53% of readers in blind tests, "slop" concerns are a red herring. But the key is original insight: "How do you tell a superintelligent being something it doesn't know already when it's been trained on the entire Internet?"

- **Dead Internet theory possibility:** Cadwallader thinks we could see a "dead Internet outcome in the next three years" where humans no longer visit websites, breaking the advertising-funded model — making social media the new platform for idea exchange and status.

- **Future of advertising in AI:** Instead of banner ads, you'll build "system prompts" as ad campaigns — targeting specific contexts and letting AI personalize the message in real-time.

- **B2B complications:** In B2B, the agent is making the purchase decision, and "we demand that our agents are objective too" — unlike humans, agents can't be "swayed by the incredible branding of Vercel."
