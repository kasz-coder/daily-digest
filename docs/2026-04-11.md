**Unsupervised Learning** — "Ep 84: OpenAI's Chief Scientist on Continual Learning Hype, RL Beyond Code, & Future Alignment Directions"

OpenAI Chief Scientist Ako Paioki sits down for a deep dive on the future of AI. The conversation covers what's actually happening with continual learning (it's not ignored — it's what they're building toward), the longer-term alignment challenges around generalization and values, and where the research frontier is heading. They discuss what it takes to make long-running agents work, OpenAI's progress in AI for science, how companies should think about model building right now, when to use reinforcement learning, and how harness evolution will impact development. Paioki also shares insights from his research on alignment and math competitions, plus what's happening at OpenAI's focusing moment and how he runs his research team.
