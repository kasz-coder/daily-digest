The Takeaway: Mistral just dropped Voxtral TTS, a speech generation model that's competitive with the best while being dramatically cheaper—a sign that audio AI is moving toward efficiency as a primary differentiator.

Guillaume Lample (Chief Scientist) and Pavan Kumar Reddy (leading audio research) joined Latent Space to announce Voxtrail TTS, Mistral's first speech generation model. This extends their audio strategy—they previously released Voxtral for transcription (ASR), added multilingual support and features like context biasing and real-time transcription, and now they're moving into speech generation. The model supports nine languages and runs on a small 3GB model that's both fast and cost-effective, priced at a fraction of competitors. They'll also be releasing the model weights. This launch positions Mistral to compete in the voice AI space alongside OpenAI's Voice Engine and others.
