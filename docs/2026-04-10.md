## Podcasts

**AI & I by Every: We Gave Every Employee an AI Agent. Here's What Happened.** — Every's COO Brandon and Head of Platform Willie share their experience deploying OpenClaw to their entire team. Key insights: agents become mirrors of their owners' personalities and expertise areas; a parallel AI org chart forms naturally as people delegate to specialized agents; the "ant death spiral" problem occurs when agents keep responding to each other in group chats. Brandon's rule: if something is already written down or documented, always ask the agent, not the human. They also launched PlusOne, a hosted OpenClaw product.
