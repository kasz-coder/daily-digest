**AI & I by Every: "We Gave Every Employee an AI Agent. Here's What Happened."**

The Takeaway: Giving every employee their own AI agent creates a personal relationship where the agent becomes a reflection of the person — transforming how work gets done.

If you're known for shipping fast, your claw becomes known for that too — and people trust it.
